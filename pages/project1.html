Image-Audio Converter

A Python tool that converts audio to and from images by reading RGB pixel data as audio samples. This mapping between visual and audio enables interesting transformations of the image through use of audio effects. The tool also allows for adding microphone input to the audio version while it is playing, allowing the user to add their own mark to an image.

Technical Approach

The conversion process flattens RGB image data from three dimensions into one and interprets it directly as audio sample data. This creates a mapping where the brightness of different color values become audio samples

Audio Effects as Visual Transformations

By applying audio effects to the converted sound and reconverting to images, various visual effects can be created.

Reverb Effect

Reverb creates ghostly echoes of the image.

High-Pass Filter

A high pass filter eccentuates the edges of the image.

Octave Up

Doubling the frequency of every pitch in the image leaves only white lines in the shape of the image.
